Uber NYC Pricing Model
Linear Regression Analysis

An end-to-end analysis about finding out the best pricing model for uber booking in New York City to increase the revenue by doing Linear Regression to find out the impact of each variables of uber booking, evaluate the existing pricing model (High Fare and Discounted Fare application), do impact analysis and give recommendation of the best uber booking pricing 11.5%. This analysis resulting a better model of uber pricing that is proven to increase 11.5% revenue.
